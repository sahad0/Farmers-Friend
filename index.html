What we are...?

Our mission is to equip, edu­cate, and inspire grow­ers to change the world
through regen­er­a­tive agriculture! rec­om­mend Farmers Friend for any­one who
wants to stay on the cut­ting edge of mar­ket grow­ing.Their prod­ucts increase
our effi­cien­cy, so we earn high­er prof­its with less work. We are a forward
thinking, visionary, Tennessee-based startup with a growing team of innovators,
manufacturers, and designers. We exist to develop tools to make 21st century
small farms more efficient and profitable.


We know small farming because we are a farm-based business. Every day we see
the fields, hear the challenges, and listen to the ideas of those who are farming
for their livelihood. This is the inspiration and motivation for all our product
development. All our tools and supplies are trialled and tested in real farm conditions.
Our goal is to continue to develop tools and supplies that make 21st century small farms
more efficient and profitable. Your success at farming becomes our success as we grow
together to meet the challenges and opportunities of the future.
